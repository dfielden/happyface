Welcome!

In the summer of 2021, Happyface had a brilliant idea - why not bring the happiness he continually experiences to the seas? Thus Happyface Enterprises was born.

Our mission is to spread happiness throughout the world by combining the natural joy of the Happyface with innovative high quality design, sure to make every sailing a joy.

For our launch, we are proud to present our patented line of Happyface nautical steering wheels which are sure to bring you happiness at every turn! Be sure to visit our site regularly as we have many more exciting products in the pipeline.
